This official creator page never asks for payment, passwords, crypto wallets, bank info, credit card info, or PayPal login. If anything looks suspicious, stop and ask in Discord first.
⚡ Quick answers before asking
Is this link safe?
Use only the official link posted by admins. This page never asks for money, passwords, wallets, bank info, credit card info, or PayPal login.
Should I reply to MochiBot?
No. MochiBot only sends reminders. Please do not reply to the bot.
Where should I ask questions?
Check FAQ first. If you still need help, post in 🎫｜town-service-station.
Why are my points not updated?
Submissions stay pending until monthly scoring is finished. Mochi will broadcast important updates.
Why can't I redeem yet?
Redemption opens after scoring. Pending rewards reserve points until admin processes them.
